# Day-1: Fundamentals of AI, ML, and DL

> **AI is Good**

## 🎓 Learning Objectives
Today's session focused on establishing a solid foundation in Artificial Intelligence and its subfields through interactive activities.

### 🧠 Concepts Covered
- **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**: The broad concept of machines being able to carry out tasks in a way that we would consider "smart".
- **Machine Learning (ML)**: A subset of AI that focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn, gradually improving its accuracy.
- **Deep Learning (DL)**: A specialized subset of ML based on artificial neural networks with multiple layers (hence "deep").

### 🛠️ Hands-on Activities
We utilized various interactive platforms to visualize these concepts:
- **Teachable Machine**: Understanding how models are trained with data.
- **Quick, Draw!**: Exploring how neural networks recognize patterns.
- **AI for Oceans**: Learning about data labeling and bias in AI.
