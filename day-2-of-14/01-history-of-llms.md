## 📜 Brief History of Transformers
Today's session explored the pivotal moments in AI history that led to the current Generative AI revolution.

### 🚀 Key Milestones
- **2017**: The release of the revolutionary paper **"Attention is All You Need"** by researchers from **Google Canada**. This introduced the **Transformer architecture**, moving away from RNNs and LSTMs.
- **2018**: **GPT-2** was released as an open-source model, showcasing the potential of large-scale unsupervised learning.
- **2018**: **BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers)** was introduced by Google, significantly improving performance on NLP tasks by looking at text in both directions.
