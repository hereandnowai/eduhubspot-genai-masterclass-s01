# Master Generative AI & Prompt Engineering: EduHubSpot Special Edition

> **AI is Good**

Welcome to the ultimate guide for mastering **Generative AI** and **Prompt Engineering**, specifically designed for learners at **EduHubSpot**. This project serves as a comprehensive resource to empower students and professionals with the skills needed to navigate and excel in the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence.

## 🚀 Project Overview

In collaboration with **Ruthran Raghavan, Chief AI Scientist**, this repository provides hands-on tutorials, practical exercises, and advanced strategies to help you harness the power of GenAI. Whether you are a beginner looking to understand the basics or an expert aiming to refine your prompting techniques, this course covers it all.

### 🎯 Key Learning Objectives

- **Understanding GenAI**: Foundations of Large Language Models (LLMs) and how they work.
- **Advanced Prompt Engineering**: Techniques like Chain-of-Thought, Few-Shot Prompting, and Iterative Refinement.
- **Contextual Awareness**: Learning how to provide the right context for high-quality AI outputs.
- **Ethics in AI**: Developing AI solutions that are responsible, safe, and aligned with human values.
- **Real-world Applications**: Building automation workflows and creative content using AI.

## 🛠️ Getting Started

1. **Explore the Modules**: Navigate through the folders to find structured lessons on different AI topics.
2. **Hands-on Practice**: Use the provided prompt templates and scripts to test your skills in the VS Code environment.
3. **Stay Updated**: AI moves fast! Follow our updates to stay ahead of the curve.
