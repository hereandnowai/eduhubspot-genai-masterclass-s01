## 🏗️ How Transformers Work
This module focused on the internal mechanisms of Large Language Models (LLMs) and how they process information.

### 🧠 The Transformer Mechanism
- **Self-Attention**: The core innovation allowing models to weigh the importance of different words in a sentence relative to others, regardless of their position.
- **Encoder vs. Decoder**: Understanding the roles of the encoder (context understanding) and the decoder (output generation).

### 🔮 How an LLM Predicts the Next Word
1. **Tokenization**: Converting text into smaller units (tokens).
2. **Contextual Encoding**: Analyzing the relationship between tokens via self-attention layers.
3. **Probability Distribution**: The model generates a list of potential next tokens and assigns probabilities based on learned patterns.
4. **Sampling & Generation**: Choosing the most likely token (or a variant based on temperature) to form the final output.
